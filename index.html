COVID-19 School Data Hub

A national database to inform policy and research about the COVID-19 pandemic and students in U.S. public schools

The COVID-19 School Data Hub (CSDH) aims to provide a platform for educators, policymakers, and researchers to understand the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on students and how they learn in schools.

Our Data

Data collected for each state through the COVID-19 School Data Hub includes school- or district-level information on the following: Instructional model (e.g., virtual, hybrid, in-person) Enrollment by instructional model COVID-19 case counts among students and/or staff
