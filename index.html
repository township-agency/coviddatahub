COVID-19 School Data Hub

A national database to inform policy and research about the COVID-19 pandemic and students in U.S. public schools

The COVID-19 School Data Hub (CSDH) aims to provide a platform for educators, policymakers, and researchers to understand the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on students and how they learn in schools.

Our Data

Data collected for each state through the COVID-19 School Data Hub includes school- or district-level information on the following:

  • Learning model (e.g., virtual, hybrid, in-person)
  • Enrollment by learning model
  • COVID-19 case counts among students and/or staff.

Additional public and private resources will be linkable with these data. The full website (including accessible data) will launch in August, 2021.
